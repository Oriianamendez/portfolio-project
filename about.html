Hi I am Oriana Mendez

Front end Developer, based in Sweden.

Hi there! I'm Oriana Mendez, originally from Venezuela but now I live in Stockholm, Sweden. I graduated as a physiotherapist, but about a year and a half ago, I decided to switch careers and learn about web development. My journey began with a focus on both back end and front end development, but I found my true passion lies in front end, which is all about making websites look great and work smoothly.

I've been learning a lot through a website called SheCodes, specializing in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Responsive Design, Bootstrap, and API integration. At this moment I am learning React and doing some personal projects. When I'm not coding, I love reading, traveling, and doing crafts.

In my past jobs, I've worked both by myself and with teams. Most of them were customer-facing roles. Overall, I'm just excited to keep learning and growing in the world of web development!
