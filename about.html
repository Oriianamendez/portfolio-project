Hi I am Oriana Mendez

Full-Stack JavaScript Developer, based in Sweden.

Hi there! I'm Oriana Mendez, a Full-Stack Developer with a passion for front-end design, originally from Venezuela and now based in Stockholm, Sweden. My career journey began in physiotherapy, but driven by curiosity and a desire for new challenges, I made a bold switch to web development about a year and a half ago. This transition has allowed me to combine my detail-oriented nature and problem-solving skills with a newfound love for coding.

I've honed my skills through platforms like SheCodes, specializing in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Responsive Design, Bootstrap, and API integration. In addition to my self-driven learning, I'm currently enrolled in a rigorous bootcamp at SALT (School of Applied Technology), where I study and work full-time as a full-stack JavaScript developer. Here, I've gained hands-on experience with technologies like Next.js, Node.js, React.js, Express.js, and database management, while also collaborating in a mob programming environment.

My diverse background, which spans across physiotherapy, entrepreneurship, and tech, has equipped me with a unique perspective on problem-solving and adaptability. Having lived and worked in Venezuela, Argentina, and Sweden, I bring a deep understanding of multicultural environments and thrive in collaborative, multicultural teams. I'm fluent in Spanish and English, and proficient in Swedish, which further enhances my ability to work in diverse settings.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading, traveling, trekking and engaging in crafts. I'm excited to continue growing in the world of web development and to contribute to building innovative software that makes a meaningful impact.
